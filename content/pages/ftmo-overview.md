					<h2>Assets and Leverage</h2>				
		<p>FTMO offers CFD trading across Forex, Indices, Commodities (Metals & Energies), Stocks, and Crypto.</p><ul><li><strong>Forex:</strong> up to <b>1 : 100</b> (Normal account type)  </li><li><strong>Indices:</strong> up to <b>1 : 50</b> (Normal account type)  </li><li><strong>Metals:</strong> up to <b>1 : 30</b> (Normal account type)</li><li><strong>Crypto:</strong> <b>1 : 1</b> (or very low leverage in Swing account)</li></ul><p>Leverage may vary depending on account type (Normal vs Swing) and traded instrument.</p>		
					<h2>Commissions</h2>				
		<p>FTMO shows no standard flat “lot commission” across all instruments publicly in their FAQ (costs are built into spreads).</p><p>Spread and commission details are available per symbol on the <a href="https://ftmo.com/en/symbols/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">“Symbols” page</a>.  </p>		
					<h2>Trading Rules</h2>				
		<p>Below are the trading rules you should follow at FTMO:</p><ul><li>You must operate within the allowed risk limits for the Challenge/Verification and Funded account (specific drawdowns vary by size).</li><li><strong>Minimum trading days:</strong> 4 business days.  </li><li><strong>Forbidden Strategies:</strong> Arbitrage, latency trading, hedging across multiple accounts, and ultra-high frequency trading are prohibited.  </li><li><p><strong>Consistency:</strong> Your trading style in the verification and funded phases must reflect the strategy shown during evaluation. </p></li></ul>		
					<h2>Copy Trading</h2>				
		<p>For traders with multiple accounts:</p><ul><li><strong>Internal replication</strong> of your own strategy across multiple accounts is subject to FTMO’s risk/distribution rules.</li><li><strong>External copy-trading</strong> (traders replicating others) is heavily restricted or disallowed under “Trading According to a Real Market” rules.</li></ul>		
					<h2>News & Weekend Trading</h2>				
		<p>FTMO’s rules around news and weekend trading are:</p><ul><li><strong>Normal account type (Classic):</strong> Limits apply on holding over the weekend and around high-impact events unless you pick the Swing account.</li><li><strong>Swing account:</strong> Allows holding overnight/weekend and trading around news events, but has lower leverage (1 : 30) to compensate.  </li></ul>		
					<h2>Scaling</h2>				
		<p>Here’s the overview of FTMO scaling and account sizes:</p><ul><li><strong>Standard account sizes</strong>: up to $200,000 (for an initial account in many cases)</li><li>Traders can increase their account size via the Scaling Plan: e.g., generate 10% net profit in 4 consecutive months to increase the balance by 25%.</li><li><strong>Merging/maximum allocation across accounts:</strong> FTMO allows merging four US$100k accounts into US$400k under certain conditions (Premium Program) </li></ul>		
					<h2>EAs (Expert Advisors)</h2>				
		<p>EAs are allowed with the following caveats:</p><ul><li>You can use algorithmic strategies or EAs, provided they reflect your trading style and are consistent between evaluation and funded phases.</li><li>Using third-party EAs that replicate widely used strategies may be flagged or lead to denial of the funded account. </li></ul>		
					<h2>Payout Rules</h2>				
		<p>FTMO Payout Rules are as follows:</p><ul><li><strong>Minimum Profit:</strong> Once you access a funded FTMO account, you are eligible for the profit split (exact minimum profit for first payout varies by contract).</li><li><strong>Profit Split:</strong> Up to <b>90%</b> of net profits are paid to the trader.</li><li><strong>First Payout:</strong> Typically within <b>14 to 3Y0 days</b> after your first trade on the funded account, depending on region and contract type. </li></ul>
