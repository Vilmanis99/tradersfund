					<h2>Assets and Leverage</h2>				
		<p>FundedNext offers CFD trading across Forex, Indices, Commodities (Metals & Energies), and Crypto.</p><ul><li><strong>Forex:</strong> up to 1:100</li><li><strong>Metals & Commodities:</strong> up to 1:40</li><li><strong>Indices:</strong> up to 1:20</li><li><strong>Crypto:</strong> up to 1:2</li></ul><p>Leverage may vary depending on the account model (Evaluation, Express, Stellar, or Stellar Instant).</p>		
					<h2>Commissions</h2>				
		<p>Here is an overview of FundedNext commissions:</p><ul><li><strong>Forex & Metals:</strong> $7/lot (Stellar Instant), $5/lot (Evaluation, Express)</li><li><strong>Indices & Energies:</strong> No commission</li><li><strong>Crypto:</strong> 0.04% per trade</li></ul><p>Spreads are raw, and commission rates can vary slightly depending on the trading server and model type.</p>		
					<h2>Trading Rules</h2>				
		<p>Below are the trading rules you should follow at FundedNext:</p><ul><li><strong>Daily Drawdown Limit:</strong> 3% (1-Step), 5% (2-Step)</li><li><strong>Overall Drawdown Limit:</strong> 6% (1-Step), 10% (2-Step)</li><li><strong>Profit Target:</strong> 8% in Phase 1 and 5% in Phase 2 (Evaluation model)</li><li><strong>Minimum Trading Days:</strong> 2 for 1-Step, 5 for 2-Step</li><li><strong>Inactivity:</strong> Accounts inactive for 30 days may be closed.</li><li><strong>Forbidden Strategies:</strong> Arbitrage, latency, tick scalping, copy trading from external sources, martingale, and account mirroring are prohibited.</li><li><strong>Consistency:</strong> Traders must maintain a consistent trading style between challenge and funded phases (manual vs EA strategy switching not allowed).</li></ul>		
					<h2>Copy Trading</h2>				
		<p>For traders managing multiple accounts:</p><ul><li><strong>Internal copy trading</strong> is allowed between your own FundedNext accounts.</li><li><strong>External copy trading</strong> or social trading is not permitted.</li></ul>		
					<h2>News & Weekend Trading</h2>				
		<p>FundedNext news and weekend trading rules are as follows:</p><ul><li><strong>Evaluation, Express, and Stellar:</strong> News and weekend trading are allowed unless otherwise specified in some plans.</li><li><strong>Stellar Instant (Direct Funding):</strong> Weekend holding is allowed, but traders should avoid opening new trades during inactive market hours.</li><li>FundedNext may void profits from trades opened seconds before major high-impact events if classified as news exploitation.</li></ul>		
					<h2>Scaling</h2>				
		<p>Here’s the overview of FundedNext account sizes and scaling:</p><ul><li>Standard account sizes: $6K, $15K, $25K, $50K, $100K, $200K.</li><li>Traders can hold multiple accounts with a combined maximum of $300,000 in allocation.</li><li>With scaling, traders can reach a maximum allocation of up to $4,000,000.</li></ul>		
					<h2>EAs (Expert Advisors)</h2>				
		<p>EAs are allowed on FundedNext as long as they are your own and used consistently across challenge and funded phases. Third-party strategy EAs or signal-based bots are not permitted.</p>
