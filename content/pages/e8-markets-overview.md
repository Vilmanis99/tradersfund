					<h2>Assets and Leverage</h2>				
		<p>E8 Markets offers trading across Forex, Futures/Indices, Commodities (Metals & Energies), and Crypto.  </p><ul><li><b>Forex:</b> up to ≈ 1 : 50 (in popular evaluation models)</li><li><b>Indices/Commodities/Metals:</b> up to ≈ 1 : 30 on some account types.</li><li><b>Crypto:</b> up to ≈ 1 : 2 in many models.</li></ul><p>Leverage may vary depending on the chosen “Challenge” or “Funded” program, account size, and the asset class.</p>		
					<h2>Commissions</h2>				
		<p>Here is an overview of <b>E8 Markets</b> commissions:</p><ul><li><b>Forex:</b><ul><li>Standard spread accounts: Free</li><li>Raw spread accounts: $5 / lot</li></ul></li><li><b>Metals:</b><ul><li>Standard spread accounts: Free</li><li>Raw spread accounts: $6 / lot</li></ul></li><li><b>Indices:</b><ul><li>Standard spread accounts: Free</li><li>Raw spread accounts: $6-$12 / lot</li></ul></li><li><b>Energy:</b><ul><li>Standard spread accounts: Free</li><li>Raw spread accounts: $6 / lot</li></ul></li><li><b>Crypto:</b> $35 / lot</li></ul><p>Spreads are generally tight, and commission rates vary based on whether you choose a <b>Standard</b> or <b>Raw Spread</b> account type.</p>		
					<h2>Trading Rules</h2>				
		<p>Below are key trading rules you’ll encounter at E8 Markets:</p><ul><li>Traders must adhere to defined <strong>drawdown limits</strong> (initial, trailing, or static) and <strong>profit-target thresholds</strong> depending on the chosen evaluation model.  </li><li><strong>Minimum trading days</strong> apply. For example, some evaluations require just 1 business day, others more.  </li><li><strong>Prohibited strategies include:</strong> arbitrage, latency/exploit trading, hedging across multiple accounts, ultra-high-frequency abuse, and trading patterns inconsistent with “real market” behavior.  </li><li><strong>Consistency rule:</strong> E8 may monitor strategy consistency and impose risk limits (e.g., max ~1% risk per trade if inconsistent behaviour is detected).  </li></ul>		
					<h2>Copy Trading</h2>				
		<p>For traders with multiple accounts:</p><ul><li>E8 Markets allows <strong>internal replication</strong> of your own strategy across your accounts in some cases, but the rules restrict copying amongst multiple accounts in the evaluation phase to ensure fair assessment.</li><li><strong>External “social trading”</strong> (other traders copying your funded account) is prohibited.</li></ul>		
					<h2>News & Weekend Trading</h2>				
		<p>E8 Markets' policy for news and weekend trading:</p><ul><li>News-event trading is permitted in many accounts; E8 expressly states news trading is allowed under their <a href="https://help.e8markets.com/en/collections/9565572-general-trading-rules" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">“Rules”</a> page.</li><li>Holding positions overnight or over weekends is permitted, but traders must be aware that gaps and slippage risk apply in these periods, and risk may be higher.</li></ul>		
					<h2>Scaling</h2>				
		<p>Here’s an overview of E8 Markets account sizes and scaling options:</p><ul><li><strong>Account sizes </strong>start from <b>$5,000</b> and go up to <b>$500,000,</b> depending on challenge type.  </li><li><strong>Scaling plan:</strong> E8 offers the opportunity to grow your funded allocation by achieving performance milestones (e.g., profit targets over a set period), resulting in, e.g., 25% capital increases every 3-4 months.</li></ul>		
					<h2>EAs (Expert Advisors)</h2>				
		<ul><li>E8 Markets permits use of EAs in many of their programs, but with important caveats: each trader must maintain a consistent trading strategy, and identical EA strategies across multiple evaluation accounts may be restricted or flagged.</li><li>Use of third-party widely distributed “signal” bots are restricted.</li></ul>		
					<h2>Payout Rules</h2>				
		<p>E8 Markets' payout rules are as follows:</p><ul><li><strong>Profit Split:</strong> Up to <b>100%</b> of net profits are paid to the trader.</li><li><strong>First Payout: </strong>Some plans allow first payout as soon as <b>8 days</b> after the first trade on the funded account in certain evaluation models.</li><li><p><strong>Payout Frequency:</strong> Many programs offer “On-Demand” payout requests, and some models have bi-weekly payout access.</p></li></ul>
