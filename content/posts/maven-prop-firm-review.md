<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>How the rules actually work</strong></h2>

<p><strong>Static drawdown.</strong> 10% maximum loss anchored at starting balance — same mechanic as FTMO and FundedNext. On a $50K Maven account, the max-loss line sits at $45,000 from day one and doesn't move as you profit; equity peaks at $52,000 don't tighten the floor. This is structurally easier than the trailing rules at Topstep and MFF where the same $52K peak would push the loss line to ~$48K and lock it there.</p>

<p>Daily-loss caps follow industry-typical levels (likely 5% on the standard 2-Step) but the specific number isn't in the firm's accessible public data — the $50K daily cap projection is therefore $2,500 of intraday risk budget pending verification on the live site.</p>

<p><strong>EA / algorithmic trading allowed</strong> on MT5. TradeLocker EA support is more limited.</p>

<p><strong>Overnight holding allowed</strong>; weekend holding not confirmed — verify directly.</p>
